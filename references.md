# References

## Approximation vs Simulation

Yu, Ron Xiaolong, and Jitendra Ganju. "Sample size formula for a win ratio endpoint." Statistics in medicine 41.6 (2022): 950-963.

Lee, Se Yoon. "A Note on the Sample Size Formula for a Win Ratio Endpoint." Statistics in Medicine 44.15-17 (2025): e70165.
- The closed formula proposed by Yu and Ganju is generally lower power than exact computation
- Trials using the estimated variance will require more patients
- Re-emphasizes computational demand of power calculations
